Skiing Stats

Growing up in a family with a long history of skiing, I first started skiing at the age of 2. I started ski racing competitively at the age of 14 and still race through college for the Cornell ski team. Since my sophomore year of high school (2014-15) I started keeping track of how many runs I took each time I skied, and since 2018, I've tracked every time I ski using a GPS tracking app called Ski Tracks. The Ski Tracks app is great because I can leave it running in the background of my phone while I ski and at the end of the day see everything from the number of runs I took, my max and min elevations, the vertical feet I skied, and much more.
